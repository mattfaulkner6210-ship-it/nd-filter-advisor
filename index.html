How to use
1

Select your drone — aperture is set automatically. Your last choice is remembered.

2

Start the camera and aim at the brightest part of the sky. Tap to move the meter spot. Tap Freeze to lock the reading so you can read the screen comfortably.

3

Fit the recommended ND filter before take-off, then lock your drone in manual mode and fly.
