How to use
1

Select your drone — aperture is set automatically for fixed-lens models.

2

Set frame rate and ISO — 30fps / ISO 100 recommended for most conditions.

3

Start the camera and point toward the brightest part of the sky. Tap to move the meter spot.

4

Fit the recommended ND filter before take-off, then lock your drone in manual mode.
